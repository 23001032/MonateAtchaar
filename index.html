About Us

At Monate Atchar, we bring you the true essence of South African flavors with our handcrafted atchar. Made from the freshest mangoes, spices, and love, our atchar adds a perfect zesty kick to pap, braai, or any meal. We're passionate about quality and tradition, serving both on-campus and off-campus customers. Open every day from 8:00 to 20:00 for your convenience.

For inquiries or bulk orders, call us at 071 256 8422 or 076 562 6537. Delivery available within selected areas!
